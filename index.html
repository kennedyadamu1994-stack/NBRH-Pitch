The Problem

There's no central place to go

Finding a drop-in football game or yoga class in London means scrolling through dead Facebook groups, checking multiple WhatsApp chats, and piecing together information that's often wrong. There's no central place to go. Organisers face the same fragmentation: juggling bookings through messages, getting zero support, and dealing with platforms that charge fees eating into tight margins. The infrastructure for social sports and exercise classes is scattered and outdated. There should be one obvious place everyone goes. Right now, there isn't.

The Solution

NBRH is building that place

One platform where London's social sports and exercise classes live: properly searchable, actually bookable, always up to date. We charge fair fees that respect club margins, provide tools that help organisers grow, and connect people with sessions they'll love. No hunting across apps, no hidden costs, no treating clubs as revenue streams. Whether you're booking or running sessions, NBRH becomes the default. Make it so straightforward that anything else feels backward.

Why Now

Social fitness is booming

Social fitness is booming. Run clubs, pick-up games, group classes: activities combining movement with community are everywhere. The market's massive and spans all ages. But the infrastructure is stuck in 2015. Information scattered across Facebook, WhatsApp, outdated websites. No platform has become the definitive place for social sports and exercise classes. The opportunity is to build something as standard as Google Maps or Trainline. The demand exists. The central hub doesn't. Yet.

Traction

Platform's live. Onboarding clubs across London, building our user base. Early stage, bootstrapped, proving it works with real sessions before scaling.

Market Timing

Social fitness exploding across all demographics. Infrastructure hasn't caught up. First mover advantage is real.

Foundation First

Building trust with clubs and users before scaling. Solid foundation, then growth.
